Georgian Cultural Boxes

Bring the soul of Georgia to your doorstep — curated artisan products and heritage in a box.

What’s Inside?

Each Cultural Box is a carefully selected collection of traditional Georgian goods, made by local artisans and small producers. Perfect as a gift, cultural education experience, or personal indulgence.

  • 🇬🇪 Handwoven textiles or traditional socks
  • 🍷 Mini bottle of qvevri wine (where legal)
  • 🍯 Raw mountain honey or churchkhela (walnut candy)
  • 📜 A scroll with the history and significance of each item
  • 🎵 Optional: Access to a playlist of Georgian polyphonic music
