Why Georgia is Ideal for Crypto Investors

Tax advantages, supportive policy, low costs, and a growing community of digital pioneers.

Key Advantages of Being in Georgia

✅ Zero Tax on Crypto Gains (for Individuals)

Georgia does not tax capital gains from personal cryptocurrency trading. This makes it one of the most crypto-friendly jurisdictions in the region.

✅ Easy & Affordable Business Registration

Set up as an Individual Entrepreneur (IE) and enjoy tax rates as low as 1% on business revenue under 500,000 GEL (~£140,000).

✅ Low Cost of Living, High Quality of Life

Georgia offers affordable housing, organic food, and a vibrant expat scene — especially in Tbilisi, Batumi, and Kutaisi.

✅ Crypto-Mining Potential

Georgia has a history as a crypto mining hub thanks to its inexpensive hydroelectric power and low energy costs in some regions.

✅ Legal Stability & Openness to Innovation

The Georgian government has embraced tech, blockchain, and digital transformation in public services — including blockchain land registry systems.

✅ Lenient Visa Policy

UK and EU citizens can stay in Georgia visa-free for up to one year, making it an ideal base for longer-term projects without residency headaches.
